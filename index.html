Jellyfin, made for Apple devices

Your Jellyfin library, made for iPhone and Apple TV.

ReelFin brings your Jellyfin library to the screens you actually use with an interface that feels calm, native, and easy to trust. Browse faster, pick something quicker, and press play without feeling like you opened a web app in disguise.

Connect your server, open your library, and get back to watching. ReelFin keeps the essentials up front: bold artwork, clear actions, proper Apple TV navigation, and a layout that feels built for Apple devices from the start.
