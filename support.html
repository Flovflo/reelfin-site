ReelFin Support

What to include

Include This In Your Message

  • Your device model and iOS or tvOS version.
  • Your ReelFin app version or TestFlight build number.
  • A short description of the problem and the last action you took.
  • Whether the issue happens on one title, one library section, or your whole server.

Helpful Beta Context

If you are testing a beta build, mention whether the issue appears during sign-in, browsing, focus navigation on Apple TV, subtitle selection, playback start, resume, or background and foreground transitions.

Fastest route to a good bug report

  • Tell us whether the problem is sign-in, browsing, or playback.
  • Include the exact item name when the issue affects only one title.
  • Mention your server version if the problem looks backend-specific.
  • Include screenshots when the UI state looks wrong.
